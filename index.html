About Vendetta

A 19-year-old visual artist based in Derry City, Vendetta creates work that refuses to be ignored. Trained part-time in art at Foyle College, their practice emerged from a need to externalize the internal—to give form to feelings that words can't capture.

Working primarily with oil paints, Vendetta's portfolio spans portraiture, abstract expressionism, and experimental compositions that challenge conventional aesthetics. Each piece operates at the intersection of beauty and discomfort, inviting viewers to confront what they'd rather look away from.

The work is intentionally provocative—not for shock value, but as an honest reflection of the complexity, contradiction, and intensity of being young and alive in this moment.
